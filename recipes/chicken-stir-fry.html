Chicken Stir-fry

Description

Nigerian chicken stir-fry is a quick, colorful dish made with seasoned chicken and fresh vegetables cooked in a lightly spicy sauce. It's simple, fast, and pairs well with rice or can be eaten on its own.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (cut into small pieces)
  • Bell peppers (sliced)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Carrots (sliced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Soy sauce
  • Curry powder
  • Black pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Season chicken with curry powder, pepper, salt, and garlic.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook chicken until lightly browned.
  3. Add onions, carrots, and bell peppers.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and cook for a few minutes until veggies are tender-crisp.
  5. Remove from heat and serve hot.
